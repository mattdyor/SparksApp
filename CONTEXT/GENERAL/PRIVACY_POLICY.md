# Privacy Policy for Sparks App

*Last updated: September 1, 2025*

## Overview

Sparks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Don't Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.** Sparks is designed to work entirely offline and does not:

- Collect personal data
- Track user behavior
- Store data on external servers
- Share information with third parties
- Require user accounts or login
- Access device contacts, photos, or other personal files

## Information Stored Locally

All data generated while using Sparks is stored locally on your device:

- **Game Progress**: Your scores, completion rates, and play history
- **Settings**: Theme preferences, sound settings, and other customizations
- **Custom Content**: Any custom spinner options or flashcard sets you create

This information:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to our servers
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app
- Is not accessible to us or any third parties

## Permissions

Sparks only requests the following permission:
- **Vibration**: To provide haptic feedback during interactions (this can be disabled in settings)

We do not request access to:
- Camera or photos
- Microphone
- Location services
- Contacts
- Network/internet (the app works completely offline)

## Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data on our servers, there's no risk of your personal information being compromised through data breaches. All your data remains securely on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Sparks is safe for children. We do not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and notify users through the app or app store updates.

## Third-Party Services

Sparks does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
